Frequently Asked Questions

Is my location data secure?

+

Yes! Your location is only shared with members of your group and is never stored permanently or shared with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

How accurate is the midpoint calculation?

+

Our algorithm calculates an equidistant point from all group members with high accuracy. We account for actual travel distances rather than just straight-line distance.

Can I create multiple groups?

+

Absolutely! You can create and manage multiple groups for different friend circles, family members, or colleagues.

What types of venues can we find?

+

midpoint.place helps you discover restaurants, cafes, bars, parks, bookstores, museums, and many other types of venues. You can filter by category to find exactly what your group is looking for.

Is midpoint.place free to use?

+

Yes! midpoint.place is completely free to use for basic functionality. We have premium features available for power users who need more advanced options.

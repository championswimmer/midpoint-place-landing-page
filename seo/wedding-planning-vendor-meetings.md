# Wedding Planning Vendor Meetings — Meet Halfway

Wedding planning involves many meetings. Save time by meeting your vendors—photographers, planners, florists—at a location convenient for both parties using midpoint.place.

## Meeting Types

- Initial consultations
- Portfolio reviews
- Contract signings

## Good Spots

- Quiet coffee shops
- Hotel lobbies
- Cafes with large tables for portfolios

## Example

A couple meets their wedding photographer at a cafe halfway between the venue and the photographer's studio to review shot lists.

## FAQs

**Can we add the venue location?**  
Yes, you can find a midpoint between your home, the vendor, and the venue if needed.

**Is this professional?**  
Meeting halfway is a common courtesy in the wedding industry.

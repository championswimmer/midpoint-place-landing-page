# Brunch Spots Equidistant for Friends — Weekend Gatherings

Sunday brunch is a tradition, but driving shouldn't be a chore. midpoint.place helps you locate top-rated brunch and breakfast spots halfway between all your friends.

## What to look for

- Bottomless mimosas
- Outdoor seating

## How to find

1. Input everyone's morning location.
2. See the midpoint area.
3. Search for "brunch" or "pancakes" nearby.

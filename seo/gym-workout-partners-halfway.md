# Gym & Workout Partners — Meet Halfway to Train

Staying motivated is easier with a partner. midpoint.place helps you find a gym, track, or fitness studio that is halfway between you and your workout buddy so no one has an excuse to skip.

## Activity Ideas

- Lifting sessions at a central gym
- Running at a midway track
- Yoga classes in a central studio

## How to choose

- Check for guest pass policies
- Look for 24-hour access
- Verify parking availability

## Example

Two friends train for a marathon by meeting for long runs at a park trail exactly 10 miles from each of their houses.

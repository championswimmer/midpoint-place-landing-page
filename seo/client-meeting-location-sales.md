# Client Meeting Locations — Professional & Convenient

Respect your client's time by suggesting a meeting spot that is convenient for them. midpoint.place helps you find a professional venue halfway between your office and theirs.

## Why it matters

- Shows respect for the client's time
- Establishes a neutral ground
- Reduces travel fatigue before the pitch

## Venue Ideas

- Hotel lobbies
- Upscale coffee shops
- Business lunch restaurants

## Example

A sales rep meets a prospect at a high-end coffee shop exactly halfway between their HQs to discuss a new contract.

## FAQs

**Can I share the map with the client?**  
Yes, send them the link to show you've picked a fair spot.

**Does it work for multiple clients?**  
Yes, if you are meeting a group, find the center of everyone involved.

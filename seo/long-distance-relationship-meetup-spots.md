# Long Distance Relationship Meetup Spots — Meet Halfway

Living in different cities? Make your time together count by finding a fair, central location. midpoint.place helps long-distance couples find romantic dinner spots, hotels, or activities exactly halfway between their homes.

## Perfect for

- Weekend getaways
- Anniversary dinners

## How it works

1. Enter both of your addresses.
2. We find the geographic center.
3. Discover restaurants, parks, or hotels near the midpoint.
4. Share the location and meet up.

## Example

A couple living in different towns meets at a cozy Italian restaurant exactly 45 minutes from each person, saving driving time and maximizing date time.

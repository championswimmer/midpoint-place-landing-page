# Carpool Pickup & Dropoff Points — Commute Smarter

Sharing the ride to work or an event? midpoint.place helps you identify the most logical place to meet up, park cars, and consolidate into one vehicle.

## Best Locations

- Park and Ride lots
- Shopping center parking lots
- Gas stations near highway exits

## Why it helps

- Save gas
- Reduce vehicle wear
- Use HOV lanes

## Example

Three coworkers living in neighboring towns meet at a designated Park & Ride lot off the main highway to share the drive into the city.

## FAQs

**Is it accurate for driving?**  
Yes, it gives you a geographic center which is usually a great starting point for finding a lot.

**Can we save the spot?**  
You can bookmark the location in your maps app once found.

# Study Group Meeting Points — Central Libraries & Cafes

Working on a group project? Don't waste time commuting across campus or the city. midpoint.place finds a central library, cafe, or study hall that is accessible to all group members.

## Ideal Venues

- University libraries
- Quiet coffee shops with Wi-Fi
- Public reading rooms
- Coworking spaces

## Benefits

- Save travel time for studying
- Ensure fair contribution to travel
- Discover new productive spots

## Example

Four students living in different off-campus apartments meet at a public library branch that is a 15-minute bus ride for everyone.

# Tutoring Session Locations — Public Libraries & Safe Spots

Private tutors and students need safe, quiet, and neutral places to work. midpoint.place helps find public libraries or quiet cafes that are equidistant for both parties.

## Top Choices

- Public Libraries (Quiet zones)
- University student centers
- Bookstores with cafes

## Why meet in public?

- Safety for both parties
- Neutral environment
- Access to resources (books, Wi-Fi)

## Example

A math tutor meets a high school student at a public library branch that splits the driving distance for both parents.

## FAQs

**Is Wi-Fi guaranteed?**  
Most public libraries offer free Wi-Fi; check specific branch details.

**Is it free?**  
Libraries are free; cafes will expect a purchase.

# Hiking Trails Halfway Between Friends — Outdoor Adventures

Planning a hike with friends who live far apart? Use midpoint.place to find a trail head or nature reserve that is a fair drive for everyone.

## Adventure Ideas

- State parks
- Nature reserves
- Coastal walks
- Mountain trails

## Planning

1. Enter everyone's starting location.
2. Find the geographic center.
3. Search for "trailhead" or "state park" near that point.

## Example

A group of friends scattered across the county meets at a trailhead for a state park that is centrally located.

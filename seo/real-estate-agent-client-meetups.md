# Real Estate Agent Client Meetups — Coffee & Consults

First time meeting a potential buyer? suggest a coffee shop or meeting place that is convenient for them. midpoint.place helps agents find the perfect spot between their office and the client's current home or target neighborhood.

## Use cases

- Buyer consultations
- Document signing
- Key handovers

## Professional tips

- Choose a spot with easy parking
- Ensure it's quiet enough for discussion
- Pick a place near the target properties

## Example

An agent meets a couple looking to buy in the suburbs at a cafe in the target town, which happens to be halfway from their city apartment.

## FAQs

**Can I use this for open houses?**  
This is better for one-on-one meetings; open houses are at the property itself.

**Does it work on iPad?**  
Yes, the site is responsive and works great on tablets.
